FAQ

Short answers for compliance leads and auditors.

Do you need access to our core system?

No. We only accept CSV exports — no credentials, no API tokens, no integration risk.

How is risk scored?

Rules-first engine. We normalize headers and map synonyms so data is read correctly, then score profile and behavioural signals. Scores band to High ≥30, Medium ≥15, else Low.

What’s in the evidence pack?

clients.json, transactions.json, cases.json, program.html, and manifest.json with SHA-256 hashes. The verify page allows read-only validation and download.

Do you store our data?

Uploads are processed in memory for the session and not written to disk. Verify links expire automatically.

Can we customise rules?

Yes. For Enterprise we tune rules per sector and can add corridors or thresholds to match your program.
