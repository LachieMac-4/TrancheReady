FAQ

Do you need system credentials?
No. Upload CSVs — we do not take API access or logins.
What’s inside the evidence ZIP?
clients.json, transactions.json, cases.json, program.html, and a signed manifest.json with SHA-256 hashes.
What rules do you apply?
Explainable, rules-first: PEP/sanctions, stale KYC, residency, service types; behavioral: structuring, high-risk corridors, large domestic transfers.
How long are verify links valid?
By default, VERIFY_TTL_MIN minutes (configurable via environment variable).
