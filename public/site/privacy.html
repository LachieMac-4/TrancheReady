Privacy Policy

We process uploads in memory and do not persist CSV contents to disk or a database. Verify links are time-limited.

Data we handle

  • Uploaded CSVs (processed in memory).
  • Derived evidence files (returned to you in a ZIP).
  • Minimal logs (timestamps, route, status code).

Retention

Verify tokens expire after 60 minutes by default. We do not retain your CSV contents.
